Our Story

Where Art Meets
Gastronomy

Tucked in the heart of Civil Lines, Pablo – The Art Cafe & Lounge is Nagpur's most iconic dining destination. Inspired by the legendary artist Pablo Picasso, every corner of this space breathes creativity — from hand-painted murals to sculptural installations that transport you into a world of colour and imagination.

We believe a great meal is a work of art. Our kitchen crafts an eclectic menu spanning Asian delicacies, European classics, wood-fired pizzas, and signature cocktails — each dish a brushstroke on a culinary canvas. Whether you're here for an intimate dinner, a vibrant night out, or a lazy Sunday brunch, Pablo offers an experience that lingers long after the last course.
